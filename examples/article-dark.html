Dark mode

WeasyPrint + Flexoki

The same layout can also render with a dark Flexoki tone, useful for on-screen PDFs or alternate export variants.

Note: for print, light mode will usually be the better default. Dark mode is here because Flexoki's dark palette is genuinely nice for digital reading.

What to customize next

  • replace the sample content with your real document types
  • add your logo and brand typography
  • introduce title pages, footers, and print metadata

Accent palette

Red, orange, yellow, green, cyan, blue, purple, and magenta are all available for callouts and visual hierarchy.
