Why this exists

Most PDF pipelines either look too plain or too corporate. Flexoki gives the page a calmer editorial feel, which works especially well for essays, notes, clinical handouts, and internal briefs.

Starter idea: use this as the base for letters, reports, one-page summaries, or branded patient information sheets.
